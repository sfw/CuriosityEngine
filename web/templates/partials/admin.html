Builds a (method × problem) matrix from the journal's entries and identifies empty cells. Classifies each gap as underexplored / tried_failed / trivially_uninteresting / regulated_boundary / adjacent_but_covered. For underexplored cells, runs academic_search to confirm the gap is real; for verified gaps, enqueues investigable questions at priority 0.85. Results persist as a coverage scan — visible in the Coverage tab. {% if not gap_ready %} Locked until the journal has at least {{ gap_min_entries }} entries — below that, most empty cells are just unexplored journal, not field-level gaps. {% endif %}
